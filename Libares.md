Pin location:
ENA - Digital 2
IN1 - Analog 0
IN2 - Analog 1

ENB - Digital 3
IN3 - Analog 2
IN4 - Analog 3

Commands:
Left turn command: L298N.left (0-255, 0-255);
Turn right command: L298N.right (0-255, 0-255);
Stop command: L298N.stop (0-255, 0-255);
Idling command: L298N.forw (0-255, 0-255);
